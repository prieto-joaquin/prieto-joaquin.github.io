<h3>Art Collective CASAGRANDE</h3>
<p>I am a co-founder of Casagrande, an art and poetry collective and magazine founded in 1996. What began as a cultural magazine aimed at showcasing the artistic production of a new generation gradually evolved into a broader interdisciplinary project that has gained national and international recognition, particularly through Bombing of Poems, a large-scale public art intervention carried out in eight cities around the world.</p>
<p>Through Casagrande, I have worked across the boundaries of poetry, visual art, and performance, using unconventional formats and public interventions to revisit historical memory and engage with contemporary and recent conflicts.</p>
<p>Recent projects include Bombing of Poems: Rotterdam (2024) and Panóptico (2025). Our next intervention, Bombing of Poems: Barcelona, will take place in June 2026. More information about the collective and its projects can be found at <a href="https://casagrande-collective.org/" target="_blank">Casagrande Collective</a>.</p>
