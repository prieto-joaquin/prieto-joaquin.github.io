<h3>Art Collective CASAGRANDE</h3>
<p>I am a co-founder of Casagrande, an art and poetry collective that began as a cultural magazine aimed at showcasing the artistic production of our generation. First published in 1996, it gradually evolved into a broader interdisciplinary project that has gained national and international recognition, particularly through <a href="https://colectivo-casagrande.org/cielo_cosmos/bombardeo-de-poemas.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Bombing of Poems</a>, a large-scale public art intervention carried out in ten cities around the world.</p>
<p>Through Casagrande, together with other members of the collective, I have explored the intersections of poetry, visual art, and performance, using unconventional formats and public interventions to revisit historical memory and engage with contemporary and recent conflicts.</p>
<p>Recent projects include <strong>Bombing of Poems: Rotterdam</strong> (2024) and <strong>Panóptico</strong> (2025). Our next intervention, <strong><a href="https://colectivo-casagrande.org/cielo_cosmos/bombardeo-de-poemas/barcelona.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Bombing of Poems: Barcelona</a></strong>, will take place in June 2026. More information about the collective and its projects can be found at <a href="https://casagrande-collective.org/" target="_blank">Casagrande Collective</a>.</p>
