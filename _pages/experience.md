<h3>Art Collective CASAGRANDE</h3>
<p>In 1996, I co-founded Casagrande, a cultural magazine originally aimed at showcasing the artistic production of our generation. Over the past two decades, that initial idea has evolved into a broader project that has gained both national and international recognition—particularly through our most prominent initiative, the &ldquo;Bombing of Poems,&rdquo; which has taken place in eight cities around the world.</p>
